Our Technology

EmbrAI leverages advanced AI analysis and federated learning to enhance embryo selection without compromising patient data privacy.

Advanced AI Analysis

Our AI analyzes key morphological factors including cell number, symmetry, fragmentation, blastocyst expansion, inner cell mass quality, and trophectoderm quality based on established grading systems.

Federated Learning

Our innovative federated learning approach keeps patient data secure within each clinic while still allowing the AI model to learn and improve from diverse datasets.

Time-Lapse Analysis

The system analyzes time-lapse imaging data to track embryo development over time, providing deeper insights than single-point assessments.

Data-Driven Decisions

EmbrAI provides objective, quantifiable assessments to complement embryologists' expertise, resulting in more informed selection decisions.
